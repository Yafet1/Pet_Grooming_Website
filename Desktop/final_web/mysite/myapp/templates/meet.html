Sylvia - grew up around dog grooming. She’s been grooming professionally for over 22 years, and is exceptionally talented. She has a keen affinity for puppies … and does a great job with all the dogs she takes care of. She can sometimes be seen dancing with the dogs at her grooming station. She loves her work, and it shows. Pets at home: 4 Pekichu, an English Bulldog, a Chihuahua, and a Plots Hound.

Cathy - has been professionally grooming for over 30 years, and is a huge lover of all animals (on her farm, she has 32 pets… from dogs and cats to goats and chickens to miniature horses to full grown horses…). On any given day, you might find any of her animals inside her house (even the goats and horses!) enjoying a belly rub. She has a gentle touch and gives love to every dog she takes care of. Pets at home: 11 dogs, 1 cat, 24 chickens, goat, mini pig, 1 mini horse, 3 horses, wild turkeys, and beavers. And – she really wants to adopt a zebra.

Devin - grew up in her mom’s grooming shop in Charleston, and decided early in life that she wanted to help dogs look and feel better every day. For over 32 years, she’s done just that. She specializes in Golden Retrievers and special-needs dogs, but is experienced and talented with all breeds. Devin is detail oriented with a soft-touch and a playful disposition. And – dogs love her Pets at home: Wifi – Pit Beagle mix, Friday – Great Dane, Nacho Kitty – Maine Coon, and In Memoriam – Ipod – Great Dane lives in my heart
